How Patch Reference Hub Is Funded

Patch Reference Hub is an independent educational website created and maintained by Dave Ross in New Zealand. This page explains how the site is funded, how referral commissions work, and how commercial relationships are disclosed and handled.

Important disclosure: Dave Ross is a LifeWave Brand Partner. That means some links on this website may generate a commission if a reader chooses to make a purchase through a qualifying referral link.
Important context: Patch Reference Hub is not an official LifeWave corporate website and does not provide medical advice. The site is intended as a beginner-focused educational resource.

Why this page exists

Transparency matters, especially on a website that discusses a health-adjacent wellness product. Readers should be able to understand when a financial relationship exists, what that relationship is, and how it affects the site.

Patch Reference Hub is built to answer common beginner questions in plain language. Because the site is operated by a LifeWave Brand Partner, it is important to explain that relationship clearly and directly rather than burying it in small print.

Brand Partner relationship

Dave Ross is a LifeWave Brand Partner.

This means that if a reader chooses to purchase LifeWave products through a referral link on this website, Dave Ross may earn a commission from that purchase through the LifeWave Brand Partner program.

That commercial relationship is disclosed throughout the site so readers understand the connection between the publisher and the product being discussed.

Patch Reference Hub is operated independently and is not an official LifeWave corporate website.

How referral commissions work

Some pages on this site may include links to official LifeWave pages or related resources. If a reader chooses to make a purchase through certain qualifying links, Dave Ross may receive a commission.

These commissions help cover the basic costs of running the site, including:

  • website hosting
  • domain registration
  • content development
  • site maintenance and updates

Not every page exists to promote a purchase. Many pages are informational and are written to answer beginner questions whether or not a reader ever clicks a referral link.

Editorial independence

The purpose of Patch Reference Hub is to help readers understand common beginner questions about LifeWave X39 patches and related topics in clear, practical language.

Content on this site is based on a mix of:

  • personal experience as a beginner user of X39
  • common beginner questions
  • publicly available manufacturer materials
  • plain-language educational explanations

The existence of referral commissions does not change the site's basic editorial purpose. Articles are written to help readers understand the topic, not to pressure them into making a purchase.

When official product instructions are the best source for a factual answer, readers are encouraged to verify those details directly with the manufacturer.

What this site does not do

Patch Reference Hub is a beginner-oriented educational resource. It does not claim to replace healthcare professionals or official manufacturer instructions.

  • It does not provide medical advice.
  • It does not diagnose or treat medical conditions.
  • It does not claim that LifeWave products cure, prevent, or treat disease.
  • It does not replace guidance from qualified healthcare professionals.

Questions about funding
