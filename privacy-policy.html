Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Patch Reference Hub may collect, use, and handle information when visitors use the website. The goal of this page is to explain privacy practices in clear language.

Important note: Patch Reference Hub is an independent educational website published by Dave Ross in New Zealand. The site may use analytics and standard website tools to understand basic visitor activity and improve site performance.
Privacy questions: If you have a privacy question about this website, you can contact the publisher at dave@patchreferencehub.com.

Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to visitors who use Patch Reference Hub at patchreferencehub.com.

It applies to general browsing activity, contact requests, and other normal interactions with the website.

What information may be collected

Depending on how the site is used, Patch Reference Hub may collect limited information such as:

  • basic website usage data
  • browser and device information
  • IP address or general location data
  • pages visited and time spent on the site
  • referring websites or traffic sources
  • information voluntarily provided through email or contact forms

This information is generally used for website operation, security, analytics, and communication.

How information may be used

Information collected through the website may be used to:

  • operate and maintain the website
  • understand how visitors use the site
  • improve page content, structure, and usability
  • respond to messages or contact requests
  • monitor website traffic and performance
  • protect the website from abuse or technical issues

Analytics and tracking tools

Patch Reference Hub may use analytics and measurement tools to understand how visitors use the site. This may include tools such as Google Analytics or similar website measurement services.

The website may also use tools such as Google Tag Manager to manage analytics and measurement scripts used to understand website performance and visitor activity.

These tools may collect standard website usage data such as page views, session duration, traffic source information, device type, and general geographic data.

This information is used to improve the website and understand which pages are helpful to readers.

Cookies

The website may use cookies or similar technologies to support analytics, basic site functionality, and performance monitoring.

A cookie is a small file stored on a visitor's device that helps websites recognize repeat visits or remember certain technical preferences.

Visitors can usually control cookies through their browser settings.

Email and contact information

If a visitor contacts Patch Reference Hub by email or through a contact form, the information provided may be used only to respond to the message, handle the request, or maintain normal communication related to the website.

Contact information is not sold to third parties.

Third-party links

Patch Reference Hub may link to third-party websites, including official manufacturer pages, social platforms, or related resources.

Once a visitor leaves this website, Patch Reference Hub is not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites. Visitors should review the privacy policies of those third-party sites separately.

Data security

Reasonable steps may be taken to protect website information, but no internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Visitors should avoid sending sensitive personal or medical information through ordinary website contact methods unless they are comfortable doing so.

Children's privacy

Patch Reference Hub is not intended for children under the age of 13, and the site does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If someone believes that personal information from a child has been provided through the site, they can contact the publisher and request review or removal where appropriate.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time if the website changes, if tools or analytics practices change, or if wording needs to be clarified.

When a meaningful update is made, the review date on this page may also be updated.
