About Patch Reference Hub

Patch Reference Hub is an independent educational website focused on beginner questions about LifeWave X39 patches. The purpose of the site is to answer common questions clearly, without exaggerated claims or unnecessary complexity.

The site focuses on practical topics such as placement, wear time, and early routine expectations. It also separates manufacturer descriptions, personal experience, and general educational explanation so readers can understand the difference.

About the publisher

Dave Ross is the publisher of Patch Reference Hub and a LifeWave Brand Partner based in New Zealand.

Dave began researching and using X39 in early 2025. This site was created to document beginner questions, organise recurring topics, and provide a clear reference point for new readers.

This website is based on direct usage experience, publicly available manufacturer materials, and clearly labelled educational explanations.
