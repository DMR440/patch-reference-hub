Quick answers for beginners

Where do people commonly place X39?

Common starting placement includes the back of the neck, often referenced as GV14, and the lower abdomen. These locations are frequently referenced in LifeWave materials and user discussions. For beginners, the main goal is to choose one starting point and keep the routine consistent long enough to evaluate it clearly.

Read the full placement guide

How long do people usually wear it?

LifeWave states patches may be worn for up to 12 hours before discarding. A common beginner schedule is to apply the patch in the morning and remove it later the same day. Always confirm current instructions on the official product or placement guide.

Read the wear-time guide

What is the first week or month usually like?

The first 30 days are usually about building a repeatable daily routine, not immediate dramatic conclusions. Many beginners keep the same wear window, use one starting placement, and focus on consistency before making changes.
