The product referenced on this site

The product most often referenced in discussions about infrared light therapy patches is LifeWave X39.

LifeWave X39 is marketed as a non-transdermal wearable patch, meaning it does not introduce substances, drugs, or supplements into the body.

Non-transdermal, in plain language

  • No drugs, supplements, or chemicals are absorbed
